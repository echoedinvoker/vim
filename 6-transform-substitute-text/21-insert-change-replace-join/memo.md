## **Replace**

### _R_

> Pressing R will enter replace mode, which will overwrite any word in this mode.

![Alt replace whole line](pic/13.jpg)

![Alt R](pic/14.jpg)

### _r_

> Replaces only one word and automatically returns to normal mode.
